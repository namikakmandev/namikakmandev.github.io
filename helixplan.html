How it works — in plain words

1. Baseline. The tool fits a growth trend plus quarterly seasonality to your history (a log-linear regression with multiplicative seasonal factors), then projects 5 years forward. The three method chips apply different trend damping — full growth, damped, or conservative. They are simplified stand-ins for the Holt-Winters / ARIMA family, honest about what they are.

2. Honest fit statistics. The error figure shown is genuinely backtested: the model is fitted on your history excluding the last four quarters, those four quarters are predicted, and the average percentage miss (MAPE) is reported. No invented accuracy numbers.

3. Adjustments. On top of the statistical baseline you layer what statistics cannot know: a loss-of-exclusivity event with exponential erosion, a demand uplift from a chosen year, and quarter re-phasing that moves weight between Q1 and Q4 without changing the full-year total. Base / Management / Downside scenarios scale these assumptions.

4. Privacy. Everything runs in your browser. Pasted data is never sent anywhere — there is no server, no analytics on your numbers, nothing to upload. The one optional exception is the AI commentary button: clicking it sends the summary figures shown on screen (annual totals, growth percentages and your settings) to an AI service to draft the text — never your pasted quarterly values, and it asks you to confirm first when you are on your own data.
