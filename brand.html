Brand Colors

The official, locked palette. These colors must be used consistently on all deliverables — website, spreadsheets, documents, presentations and any future projects.

Accent — Green

#19C37D
Primary brand color. Buttons, key highlights, positive values.

Accent — Blue

#2F9BFF
Secondary accent. Gradients, links, secondary highlights.

Accent — Orange

#FF6500
Tertiary accent. Call-to-action, warnings, emphasis. Use sparingly.

Usage rule

Corporate application (60-30-10): ~90% of every layout is the dark neutral base. Blue #2F9BFF is the single primary accent — used solid (no gradients) for buttons, links and all functional UI; it carries the trustworthy, consultancy-standard tone. Green #19C37D is the signature accent — used sparingly and deliberately on the logo and hero headline only, to stay distinctive without competing with blue. Orange #FF6500 is tertiary — rare punctuation for calls-to-action, warnings or emphasis. Red #E5484D is a semantic color reserved strictly for negative financial values (losses) — never a brand/decoration accent. Dark theme for marketing pages (home, projects, about); a light surface (soft gray / white) is allowed for interactive tools and data views, where it improves readability — accents stay identical. Do not introduce other accent colors without updating this page first — this document is the single source of truth.
