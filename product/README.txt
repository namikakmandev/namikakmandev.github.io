THE ONE-PAGE BUSINESS REVIEW
============================
Turn a month of numbers into one page a board actually reads and
acts on. One HTML file, no install, no dependencies, no tracking —
nothing ever leaves your browser.

WHAT'S INCLUDED
- one-pager.html              the builder (open in any browser)
- ONE-PAGER-METHOD-GUIDE.txt  read this once — it's the part that
                              makes a board act, not just read
- README.txt                  this file

QUICK START (2 minutes)
1. Double-click one-pager.html — it opens in your browser.
2. Edit the form on the left:
   - Header & brand: company, title, period, prepared by, brand colour
   - KPI tiles (6): label, value, note, tone (good / bad / neutral)
   - The story: type short What / Why / So what bullets — the tool
     composes the executive sentence for you
   - Decisions (3): the decision, plus an owner and a date
   - Outlook & footer: one honest sentence
3. The A4 page on the right updates as you type.
4. Pick a layout at the top: Executive, KPI-focused, or Narrative.
5. Click "Print / Save as PDF" → choose "Save as PDF". You get one
   clean page. Send that.
"Reset to example" restores the sample content at any time.

THE METHOD MATTERS MORE THAN THE TEMPLATE
The template is the easy part. Open ONE-PAGER-METHOD-GUIDE.txt and
read it once (5 minutes): how to pick 6 KPIs ruthlessly, write the
story in the what -> why -> so what formula, and force every review
to end in real decisions instead of FYIs. That guide is why this
page gets read and acted on.

EMBED IT (optional)
To put the live builder on a site you own:
   <iframe src="one-pager.html"
           style="width:100%;height:900px;border:0"></iframe>
