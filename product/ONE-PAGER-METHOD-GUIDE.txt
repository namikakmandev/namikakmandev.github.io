THE ONE-PAGE BUSINESS REVIEW — METHOD GUIDE
===========================================
The template is the easy part. THIS is the part that makes a board
read it and act. Five minutes — read it once.

WHAT'S INCLUDED
- one-pager.html            the builder (open in any browser)
- ONE-PAGER-METHOD-GUIDE.txt  this guide

HOW TO USE THE BUILDER
1. Open one-pager.html in any browser.
2. Fill the form on the left — header, 6 KPIs, the story, 3 decisions,
   outlook. The page on the right updates as you type.
3. Set your brand colour (top of the form).
4. Click "Print / Save as PDF" → choose "Save as PDF". You get one
   clean page. Send that.

THE 6 KPIs — PICK RUTHLESSLY
Six, no more. A board does not read twelve numbers. Use the ones that
answer "are we winning, and is anything on fire":
  - one growth number (revenue / bookings)
  - one vs-plan number (variance)
  - one profitability number (margin or EBITDA)
  - one cash number (cash / runway)
  - two that matter THIS month (whatever the story is about)
Set the tone: good (green), bad (orange), neutral (grey). Honesty in
the colours builds trust faster than good numbers do.

THE STORY — 3 SENTENCES, A FORMULA
Most managers describe; leaders explain. Each line follows:
  WHAT happened  →  WHY  →  SO WHAT (is it timing, structural, fixable?)
Example:
  "Revenue grew 6% but landed 3% under plan as two deals slipped to
   Q2 — timing, not lost demand."
Rules:
  - Name the cause, not just the movement.
  - Say whether it is one-off or structural. Boards fear surprises,
    not bad numbers.
  - If something is bad, say it in line 1. Hiding it costs you more.

THE 3 DECISIONS — THIS IS THE WHOLE POINT
A report nobody acts on is wallpaper. Every review must end in at most
THREE decisions, each with an OWNER and a DATE. Test each one:
  - Is it a decision, or just an "FYI"? (Cut FYIs.)
  - Does it have a single owner? (No owner = it won't happen.)
  - Does it have a date? (No date = it won't happen.)
If you cannot name 3 decisions, the month had no decisions — say so,
and the review still took 30 seconds to read. That is success.

OUTLOOK — ONE HONEST SENTENCE
Reaffirm or change guidance, and say what has to be true for it. No
hedging paragraphs.
