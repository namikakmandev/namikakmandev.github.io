QUICK START (2 minutes)
1. Double-click one-pager.html — it opens in your default browser.
2. On the left, fill the form: header, 6 KPIs (with colour tones),
   the 3-sentence story, the 3 decisions (owner + date), outlook.
   The page on the right updates as you type.
3. Set your brand colour at the top of the form.
4. Click "Print / Save as PDF" -> choose "Save as PDF" in the print
   dialog. You get one clean A4 page. Send that to your board.

BEFORE YOU SEND IT — READ THE METHOD GUIDE
Open One-Page-Business-Review-Method-Guide.pdf. Five minutes. It is
the part that makes the difference between a report nobody reads and
a review that ends in action.
