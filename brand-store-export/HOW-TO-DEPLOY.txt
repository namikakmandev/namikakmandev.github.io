WHY NOT JUST PUT IT ON YOUR EXISTING SITE
-----------------------------------------
namikakmandev.github.io and its GitHub repo are PUBLIC under your real
name. A hidden/unlinked page there is still browsable by anyone who
finds your GitHub — including your employer. noindex only hides from
Google, not from someone reading the repo. So the store must live
somewhere with NO link back to you. That's what this export is for.

STEP 4 — (RECOMMENDED) ALSO LIST ON GUMROAD FOR DISCOVERY
---------------------------------------------------------
Lemon Squeezy is checkout-only — no marketplace, so buyers won't find
you unless you send them. Gumroad has a public "Discover" marketplace,
so it brings organic buyers WITHOUT you marketing under your name.
  1. Sign up at gumroad.com with the BRAND email.
  2. Settings > set your profile/username and display name to the
     BRAND. Payout (bank/PayPal) info is private — buyers don't see it.
  3. New product > "Digital product" > upload the same files >
     price 4.99 > publish.
  4. Enable it in Gumroad Discover (product > "Show in Discover" /
     marketplace toggle) and add the relevant category + tags
     (e.g. business, spreadsheets/templates, finance). This is the
     organic-discovery channel.
  5. Copy the Gumroad product URL into GUMROAD_URL in index.html so
     the "Also on Gumroad" button appears. Re-upload.
You can sell on both; Lemon Squeezy = your branded store link,
Gumroad = passive marketplace reach.
