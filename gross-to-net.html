Product Margin Simulator

A live gross-to-net waterfall: start from list price and walk down through rebates, discounts, COGS, OpEx and other costs to the real operating profit. Pick an industry preset or type your own numbers — the chart, the KPIs and the branded Excel export all update instantly. This mirrors the net-revenue logic I work with in commercial operations.

How the margin waterfall works
  1. Net price. Gross / list price minus Rebates and Discounts, plus any Markups or surcharges — what the customer effectively pays.
  2. Gross margin. Net price minus COGS (cost of goods sold) — the money left to run the business.
  3. Operating profit. Gross margin minus OpEx and Other costs — the true bottom line on the deal.
  4. Every percentage (gross margin %, operating margin %, markup on COGS) is expressed against net price, the way commercial teams actually read it.

The exported Excel is fully formula-driven — change any input cell and the workbook recalculates exactly like this page.
