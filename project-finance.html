ProjeFin is an illustrative project finance (PF) model for infrastructure projects. It capitalizes construction-period capex draws and interest during construction (IDC); in the operating period it models revenue ramp-up/escalation, operating cost, maintenance capex and depreciation-adjusted tax. Debt is repaid either by annuity or sculpted to a target DSCR. Outputs: unlevered Project IRR, levered Equity IRR, NPV, DSCR (min/average) and LLCR. Tax is computed with an interest shield for equity, and without interest for the project (unlevered). All calculations run in the browser, on an annual basis вЂ” this is not an official financial model or investment advice.
