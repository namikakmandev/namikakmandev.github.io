Infrastructure · Project finance · OVP macro · HTML · JS

ProjeFin — Infrastructure Project Finance

How do you price and stress-test a multi-billion-lira infrastructure deal? ProjeFin builds the full project-finance waterfall — a construction phase that capitalises interest during construction (IDC), then an operations phase with revenue ramp, escalation, opex and depreciation-based tax. Debt repays by annuity or sculpts to a target DSCR. It reports the unlevered Project IRR alongside the levered Equity IRR, NPV, min/average DSCR and LLCR, with a sources & uses panel, a DSCR profile, an equity J-curve and a year-by-year cash-flow table. Wired to Türkiye’s Orta Vadeli Program (OVP): the interest-rate path and a USD/TRY projection flow through every year, and a USD-equivalent equity return shows how much of the lira IRR is just currency depreciation. Turkish-language tool.

Poultry · Operations · Profitability · HTML · JS

KuluçkaTakip — Hatchery Operations & Profitability

Which hatch runs made money — and which site is quietly bleeding? KuluçkaTakip is a Turkish-language tracker for poultry hatcheries: log each set (eggs in, chicks out, egg cost, sale price), enter monthly running costs — wages, energy, rent, maintenance — and it allocates them across batches to show hatch rate, cost per chick and profit per batch, per month and per site. Runs multiple hatcheries side by side with a comparison board, keeps versioned backups and exports to Excel. Built for a non-technical owner: no setup, no login, data stays in the browser. A companion investment model sizes a new hatchery from capacity to payback.
